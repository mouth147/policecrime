About This Site

In recent times, it has come to light that people who are put in position of power, and great responsibility take advantage of their authority over the common civilian. With technology on the rise, people have the ability to document this abuse of power. This is a collection of all known police related deaths. The data that populates this website is updated every day to keep track of all police related events. On this page you can do a basic search of someone's name or a location (i.e. cities: Newark, Los Angeles, Chicago | states: NJ, PA, CA, WA).
Please check out the statistics page for more information on states and their crime rates.
